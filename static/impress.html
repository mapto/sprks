On its quest to expand internationally, London-based energy company Global Sparks buys electricity suppliers along the US West Coast

This rapid expansion invites fragmentation within the company

To save off further damage, Global Sparks consolidates its assets, finding waves of redundancies along the way

The company sees growth after consolidation and starts to hire once more, albeit on a limited basis

Many of these contractors are former employees who had been made redundant during consolidation

As part of these recent signings, you've been hired as Chief Information Security Officer - CISO

You task is two-fold:

  • Ensure no security weaknesses were inherited during consolidation
  • Maintain company-wide security policies that balance risks and costs
